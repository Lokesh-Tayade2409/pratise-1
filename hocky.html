basic rules of field hockey:

  1. Stick: Players can only use the flat side of their stick to play the ball. They can't use the rounded side, and they can't hold the stick above their waist during regular play.
  2. Body contact: Players can't charge, hit, shove, or trip an opponent. They also can't make excessive body contact with another player.
  3. Obstruction: Players can't shield the ball from an opponent with their body or stick. They also can't obstruct the ball by turning their back to another player and standing between the ball and that player.
  4. Tackling: Players can't tackle from behind.
  5. Fighting for the ball: Players must face their opponent head on when fighting for the ball.
